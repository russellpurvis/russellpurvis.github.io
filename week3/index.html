Site Purpose

The purpose of The Weather Ninja is to help those who wish to remain unseen move about the world, by describing what the weather will be and what they should wear in correlation. The goal of The Weather Ninja is to create a community of ninjas whose goal is to teach and master the art of getting around, by sharing their expertise on what to wear during specific conditions.
