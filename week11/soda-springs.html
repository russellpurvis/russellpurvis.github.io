Largest Man-Tamed Geyser

The only attraction in Soda Springs is a unique semi-unnatural feature downtown -- a geyser. It's been there, ready to blast skyward, since 1937 when a local man drilling in search of heated water for a swimming pool penetrated an underground chamber 315 feet down.
